Boss’s assistant

As Rice Coming has several outlets and the manager cannot be in the shop all the time, we have equipped it with an owner's assistant that allows it to view the marketing data remotely. The owner's assistant is linked to the EPos system to ensure the accuracy of the data. At the same time, this digital assistant presents sales data and rankings by different times, periods, dishes and categories.

Owners only need to scan the Epos machine QR code to log in or use the WeChat official platform to log in.

Membership management

Membership management

Rice Coming restaurants have been using our membership management system for several years, which allows customers to join the membership of Rice Coming Chian and spend promotional prices at all registered Rice Coming stores. The membership management system allows for registering, reporting losses and exchanging cards. Restaurant owners can define their own membership prices or membership discounts. The establishment of a membership system is a powerful tool to enable the restaurant chain increase customer retention.
