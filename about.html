Introduction

Trinal Genius founded in 2013, specializes in electronic point of sale systems (advanced cash register system) for the catering and retail industries. Our goal is to provide business clients with the highest quality systematic and integrated products and services through advanced technology and efficient solutions. We are dedicated to helping our customers continuously improve service efficiency and competitiveness in their industries.

Trinal Genius founded in 2013, specializes in electronic point of sale systems (advanced cash register system) for the catering and retail industries. Our goal is to provide business clients with the highest quality systematic and integrated products and services through advanced technology and efficient solutions. We are dedicated to helping our customers continuously improve service efficiency and competitiveness in their industries.

As one of the UK's leading Epos and cash register operating companies, we particularly focus on the needs of our clients in the Chinese catering industry. So far, we have clients in over a dozen cities across the UK and nearly 200 partners in London City.
